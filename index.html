Ukraine's Destiny

A shadow of it's Soviet Past

One of the biggest tragedies of the world is how the ukraine Russia war has unfolded. In one way the westen countries have been rearming ukraine and giving it entry into nato to use it as a launchpad for a future russian invasion and continued russian aggression on Ukraine's Eastern border through the breakaway republics of Luhansk and Donetsk.

Carving out a separate identity for Ukraine

Ukraine has been a country that has been under the influence of Russia for a long time. The country has been trying to carve out its own identity and move away from the shadow of its Soviet past. The war has only intensified this struggle, with many Ukrainians feeling a strong sense of nationalism and a desire to assert their independence.

Impact on the people

The war has had a devastating impact on the people of Ukraine. Thousands have lost their lives, and many more have been displaced from their homes. The economy has been severely affected, with businesses closing down and unemployment rising. The conflict has also led to a deepening divide within the country, with some regions supporting the government and others siding with the separatists.
